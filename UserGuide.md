# User Guide

This guide explains how to use ActivityHub — the campus activity calendar.

## Accessing the Application

Open a browser and go to:
```
http://<raspberry-pi-ip>/index.php
```

## Main Calendar Page

When you open the app you will see:

- **Header** — navigation links to Calendar and Team pages
- **Sidebar** — mini calendar and category filters
- **Main area** — monthly calendar grid and activity list below it

## Browsing Activities

### Calendar Grid
The main calendar shows the current month. Days with activities display colored pills showing the activity name. Click any pill to view the activity details.

### Activity List
Below the calendar is a list of all activities for the month. Each card shows:
- Activity title and category icon
- Date, time, and location
- Organizer name
- Number of people joined vs maximum capacity
- Open or Full badge

Click any activity card to view full details.

## Navigating Months

Use the **← Prev**, **Today**, and **Next →** buttons at the top right to move between months. The sidebar mini calendar also updates accordingly.

## Filtering by Category

In the sidebar, click any category (Sports, Study, Social, Arts, Tech) to filter activities. Click **All Categories** to show everything.

## Viewing Activity Details

Click any activity to open the detail panel showing:
- Full title, date, time, location
- Organizer name and category
- Description
- List of people who joined
- Join form (if activity is not full)
- Edit and Delete buttons

## Joining an Activity (RSVP)

1. Click on an activity to open its details
2. Type your name in the **"Your name to join..."** field
3. Click **Join!**
4. Your name will appear in the joined list

> Note: You cannot join the same activity twice with the same name. If an activity is full, the join form will not appear.

## Creating a New Activity

1. Click **+ New Activity** in the top right header
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** (required)
   - **Description**
   - **Category**
   - **Location**
   - **Start Date & Time** (required)
   - **End Date & Time** (required)
   - **Max Participants** (leave blank for unlimited)
   - **Your Name** (required)
3. Click **Create Activity**

## Editing an Activity

1. Click on an activity to open its details
2. Click the **✏️ Edit** button
3. Modify the fields as needed
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Deleting an Activity

1. Click on an activity to open its details
2. Click the **🗑 Delete** button
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

> Warning: Deleting an activity also removes all RSVPs associated with it.

## Team Page

Click **Team** in the header to view information about the development team members.
